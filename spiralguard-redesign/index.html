Trusted across South African industries

Wherever hoses and cables face abrasion, impact or harsh conditions, spiral guard helps keep them running.

water_drop

Hydraulic hose protection

Essential for hydraulic systems operating under high pressure. Prevents abrasion from contact with machinery, protects against cuts from sharp edges, and shields hoses from environmental damage, extending hose life and reducing the risk of costly failures.

cable

Cable protection

Electrical cables and wiring require protection from mechanical damage, abrasion and environmental factors. Spiral guard wrapping provides a flexible protective layer that moves with the cable while maintaining protection.

precision_manufacturing

Manufacturing & machinery

Manufacturing and processing machinery often has exposed hoses and cables vulnerable to damage. Spiral guard prevents wear from moving parts and reduces maintenance requirements, keeping production lines running.

agriculture

Agriculture

Farm machinery operates in harsh outdoor conditions with exposure to debris, weather and continuous movement. Spiral guard shields hydraulic hoses and electrical cables from field hazards, extending equipment life.

Mining & construction

Mining and construction sites present some of the toughest operating conditions. Spiral guard withstands extreme abrasion, impact and environmental exposure on excavation, drilling and material transport equipment.

Industrial equipment
