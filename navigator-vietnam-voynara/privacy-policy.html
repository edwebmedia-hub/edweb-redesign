Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect the information you share with Navigator Vietnam.

Last updated: 1 July 2026. This policy explains what personal information Navigator Vietnam collects and how we use it. By using our website and services, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

When you contact us or make an enquiry, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address and phone number
  • Travel preferences and destination interests you share with us
  • Any details you include in your message

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to enquiries and plan your trip
  • Send booking details, itineraries and travel information
  • Improve our services and communication

How We Protect It

We take reasonable measures to safeguard your information and never sell it to third parties. We share details only with the local partners and guides needed to deliver your trip.

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing info@navigator-vietnam.com.
