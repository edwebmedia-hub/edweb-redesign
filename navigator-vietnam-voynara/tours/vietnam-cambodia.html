Vietnam & Cambodia: From Ha Long Bay to Angkor Wat

16 days across two extraordinary countries, from the karsts of Ha Long Bay and the caves of Phong Nha to sunrise over Angkor Wat.

Hanoi Ha Long Bay Pu Luong Phong Nha Hue Hoi An Siem Reap

Overview

Experience the very best of Southeast Asia on this unforgettable 16-day journey through Vietnam and Cambodia. From the bustling streets of Hanoi and the emerald waters of Ha Long Bay to the tranquil rice terraces of Pu Luong, the spectacular caves of Phong Nha, the imperial history of Hue, the lantern-lit charm of Hoi An, and the magnificent temples of Angkor Wat, this itinerary showcases the incredible diversity of two remarkable countries. Designed for travellers seeking the perfect balance of culture, nature, history and authentic local experiences, this journey combines iconic highlights with hidden gems rarely found on standard tours. Whether it's your first visit to Southeast Asia or you're looking for a comprehensive journey through two extraordinary destinations, this itinerary offers memories that will last a lifetime.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore Hanoi aboard a vintage Army Jeep or motorbike
  • Discover the secrets of Vietnam's famous coffee culture
  • Cruise through the spectacular limestone islands of Ha Long Bay overnight
  • Trek through the breathtaking rice terraces of Pu Luong Nature Reserve
  • Visit local Thai ethnic minority communities
  • Explore the magnificent caves of Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park
  • Discover Vietnam's wartime history at the Vinh Moc Tunnels and DMZ
  • Explore the Imperial City of Hue
  • Drive across the spectacular Hai Van Pass
  • Join a hands-on Vietnamese cooking class in Hoi An

  • Visit Cambodia's inspiring APOPO HeroRATs Centre
  • Watch sunrise over the magnificent Angkor Wat
  • Explore the incredible temples of the Angkor Archaeological Park

Itinerary

Day 1

Welcome to Hanoi

Arrive in Vietnam's vibrant capital and settle into your hotel in the Old Quarter. Meet your guide and enjoy a welcome dinner while experiencing your first taste of authentic Vietnamese cuisine.

Day 2

Discover Hanoi

Explore Hanoi aboard a vintage Army Jeep or Motorbike, visiting famous landmarks including Train Street, Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, West Lake, and the Old Quarter before learning the art of Vietnamese coffee during an interactive coffee workshop.

Day 3

Ha Long Bay Cruise

Travel to the spectacular UNESCO World Heritage Site of Ha Long Bay and board your overnight luxury cruise. Explore hidden caves, kayak through limestone islands, enjoy swimming opportunities, and watch the sunset over one of the world's most beautiful seascapes.

Day 4

Ha Long Bay to Pu Luong

Enjoy your final morning cruising through Ha Long Bay before travelling inland to the peaceful mountains of Pu Luong Nature Reserve, home to spectacular rice terraces and traditional Thai villages.

Day 5

Trek Through Pu Luong

Spend the day trekking through lush valleys, picturesque rice terraces and remote villages while learning about the traditions and daily life of the local Thai ethnic minority communities.

Day 6

Journey to Phong Nha

Travel south to the peaceful village of Phong Nha, surrounded by dramatic limestone mountains and the gateway to one of Asia's most impressive cave systems.

Day 7

Explore Phong Nha

Visit the famous Phong Nha Cave, cruise along the Son River, enjoy local cuisine, and experience the relaxed countryside atmosphere of this beautiful UNESCO National Park.

Day 8

Paradise Cave & Commander Cave

Discover two of Vietnam's most remarkable cave systems while learning about their incredible geological formations and important role during the Vietnam War.

Day 9

Vietnam's Wartime History

Travel towards Hue via the Vinh Moc Tunnels and the former Demilitarized Zone, gaining a fascinating insight into Vietnam's modern history.

Day 10

Imperial Hue

Explore the magnificent Imperial City, ancient citadel and rich heritage of Vietnam's last royal dynasty before enjoying free time to discover Hue's famous cuisine and vibrant markets.

Day 11

Scenic Journey to Hoi An

Drive across the breathtaking Hai Van Pass, visit the towering Lady Buddha overlooking Da Nang, and arrive in the enchanting UNESCO-listed Ancient Town of Hoi An.

Day 12

Hoi An Cooking Experience

Visit Hoi An's colourful local market before enjoying a hands-on Vietnamese cooking class and a traditional bamboo basket boat ride through the peaceful coconut forests.

Day 13

Vietnam to Cambodia

Transfer to Da Nang Airport for your short flight to Siem Reap, Cambodia, where you'll begin the second chapter of your Southeast Asian adventure.

Day 14

Discover Siem Reap

Visit the inspiring APOPO HeroRATs Centre to learn about Cambodia's remarkable landmine clearance programme before exploring the fascinating Lotus Silk Farm and discovering traditional Khmer craftsmanship.

Day 15

Sunrise at Angkor Wat

Wake before dawn to witness one of the world's most unforgettable sunrises over Angkor Wat before spending the day exploring the magnificent temples of the Angkor Archaeological Park, including Bayon, Ta Prohm and Angkor Thom City.

Day 16

Farewell Cambodia

Enjoy breakfast before transferring to Siem Reap International Airport for your onward journey home.

Travel Notes

  • Best travel season: October to April
  • Comfortable walking shoes are recommended for sightseeing and light trekking
  • Modest clothing covering shoulders and knees is required when visiting temples, including Angkor Wat and the Imperial City of Hue
  • Vietnam uses the Vietnamese Dong (VND), while Cambodia accepts both the Cambodian Riel (KHR) and US Dollar (USD)
  • Travel insurance is compulsory
  • This itinerary includes a combination of cultural sightseeing, scenic walks and light trekking, suitable for most travellers with a moderate level of fitness
