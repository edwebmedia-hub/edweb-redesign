Frequently Asked Questions

Is it easy to find vegetarian food in Vietnam?

Vietnam is generally very vegetarian-friendly, though vegan options can be more limited because of the fish sauce used in many dishes. Most desserts, however, tend to be vegan-friendly by default.

Are your trips beginner-friendly?

Yes! We offer beginner, intermediate and expert-level adventures. Each trip lists difficulty details so you can choose what suits you best.

How much should a taxi from the airport cost?

In Ho Chi Minh City, a taxi from Tan Son Nhat Airport costs between 120,000 and 170,000 VND and takes 30 to 50 minutes. In Hanoi, a fixed fare from Noi Bai Airport is 330,000 VND, typically a 45 to 60 minute journey depending on traffic.

Is it safe to travel alone?

Vietnam is considered one of the safest destinations for solo female travelers in Southeast Asia. Locals are typically respectful and violent incidents are rare. In cities like Ho Chi Minh City, stay alert to petty crimes such as bag snatching by motorbike riders.

Which taxi companies should I use?

To avoid scams, stick with trusted companies like Taxi Group (Hanoi), Vinasun (Ho Chi Minh City), and Mai Linh (both cities). Ride-hailing apps such as Grab, Bee and GoViet are also available with a Vietnamese SIM card.

What are the most common health issues?

Most complaints are mild, like colds or stomach issues. During hot weather, heat-related illnesses such as heatstroke and dehydration can occur, so stay hydrated and use sunscreen regularly.

How much does it cost to rent a motorbike?

Motorbike rental ranges from 6 to 12 USD per day depending on the bike's model and quality. You may need to leave your passport or a deposit for rentals longer than a week. Always wear a helmet and drive cautiously.

How can I start learning Vietnamese?

Begin with the basics like "Xin chào" (Hello / Goodbye) and "Cảm ơn" (Thank you). VietnamesePod101 has helpful word lists and phrases for travelers.
