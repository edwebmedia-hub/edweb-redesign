The Brief

Turn eight branches into one easy ordering flow

Crazy Daizy bakes cakes, cupcakes and savouries across eight pickup locations in Cape Town, so the site needed a full WooCommerce catalogue with category filtering, plus a dedicated page for every branch with its own hours, contact details and map link. Since every order is collected in person rather than delivered, the whole checkout flow was built around picking a store, not a shipping address.
