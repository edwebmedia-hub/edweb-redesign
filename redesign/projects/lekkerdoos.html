The Brief

Sell a wine box without losing the personality

Lekker Doos needed a WooCommerce store that could handle their once-off curated wine box, real product reviews, and the operational detail of order-cutoff dates, while still feeling like the fun, community-driven brand behind it. Bold display type, a warm red-and-cream palette and founder faces up front do the work of making the box feel personal rather than transactional.
